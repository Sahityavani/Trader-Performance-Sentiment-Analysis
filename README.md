# Trader-Performance-Sentiment-Analysis
Analysis of trader performance based on market sentiment (Fear vs Greed).

# Trader Performance vs Market Sentiment

##  Objective
Analyze how market sentiment (Fear vs Greed) impacts trader behavior and profitability.

---

##  Datasets Used
- Bitcoin Market Sentiment (Fear/Greed Index)
- Historical Trader Data (Hyperliquid)

---

##  Methodology
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Merging datasets on date
- Feature engineering (PnL, trade frequency, etc.)
- Sentiment-based analysis
- Trader segmentation

---

##  Key Insights
1. Traders earn higher profits during Extreme Greed periods.
2. Trading activity is highest during Fear periods.
3. High activity during Fear does not translate into higher profits.
4. Emotional trading negatively impacts performance.

---

##  Strategy Recommendations
1. Reduce overtrading during Fear periods to minimize losses.
2. Use trend-following strategies during Greed periods for better profitability.
